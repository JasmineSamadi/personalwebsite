Quantitative Research Analyst

In this project, I conducted quantitative research by analyzing historical data from April 23rd, 2019 to April 23rd, 2024. This data focused on Novo Nordisk and NVIDIA stocks sourced from Yahoo Finance.

We developed R code, utilizing mean excess plots and estimators (including Pickands, Hills, and Moment Estimators) to assess tail behavior and estimate shape parameters.
Our outcome revealed a heavy-tailed distributions for both stocks.

This project entailed using Python to determine the relationship between a UN policy, addressing more women in the decision making process, and Gender Inequality Index (GII).
